Where to Eat

Maxwell Foodhall

Kadayanallur St, Singapore 069184

Classical Singaporean foodcourt in the heart of Chinatown. It is a full-circle experience filled with local dishes from various cultures. It is one of the oldest food courts, feeding locals and tourists since the 1940s. It offers a variety of budget-friendly and delicious options - perfect for students living on a budget.
